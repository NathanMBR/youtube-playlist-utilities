1. DESCRIPTION OF THE APPLICATION
================================================================================

YouTube Playlist Utilities is a free, open-source desktop application for
Windows and Linux. It allows you to connect your Google account,
inspect your YouTube playlists for unavailable or non-public videos, and
remove or replace those videos at your request.

The Application communicates directly with Google's servers on your behalf.
It has no backend server of its own and does not store your data outside of
your local device.
